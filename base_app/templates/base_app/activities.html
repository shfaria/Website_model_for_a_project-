    Prepare and Launching of Web Portal
    Female Engineers in each country will actively participate to develop a web portal for the project under NORDUnet mentorship. Female Interns, once they join, will take the responsibility of making regular update of the portal.


    Development of a centralized Dashboard
    In order to display the project activities in terms of conduction of number of meetings and classes conducted by each NREN with all associated data (duration, participants, used devices, operating systems and others) a “Dashboard” will be developed by the Female Engineers under NORDUnet Mentorship.
    The Dashboard will collect meeting/class CDRs (Call Detail Recors) from each inpidual NREN and show it graphically in the Dashboard similar to the available “Dashboard” in Zoom portal.
